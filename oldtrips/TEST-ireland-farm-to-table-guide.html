Driving in Ireland

  • Important: Drive on the LEFT side of the road
  • International Driving Permit is NOT required for U.S. license holders
  • Rural roads are often narrow with stone walls - drive cautiously
  • Speed limits: Urban areas (50 km/h), Regional roads (80 km/h), National roads (100 km/h)
  • Zero tolerance for drinking and driving
  • Most rental cars are manual transmission - request automatic if needed
  • Navigation apps may not work in remote areas - have a backup map

Local Customs

  • Tipping: 10-15% in restaurants if service not included, taxi drivers (round up fare), hotel staff (€1-2 per bag)
  • Pub Etiquette: Order and pay at the bar, tipping not expected but appreciated
  • Greetings: Handshakes common, casual and friendly atmosphere
  • Weather Talk: Common conversation starter
  • "Irish Time" can be relaxed - expect some flexibility in schedules

Wi-Fi & Connectivity

Most accommodations offer free Wi-Fi. Public Wi-Fi available in most pubs, cafes, and visitor centers. Mobile coverage varies in rural areas.
