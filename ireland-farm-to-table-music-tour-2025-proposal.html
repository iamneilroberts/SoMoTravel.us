ITINERARY HIGHLIGHTS

Day 1-2: Dublin & Wicklow Mountains Nov 2-3

Begin your Irish adventure with two days exploring Dublin and the stunning Wicklow Mountains. Visit the historic Rock of Dunamase, experience the majestic Powerscourt Waterfall, and explore the 6th-century monastic settlement at Glendalough. Each evening, enjoy authentic Irish music sessions at Dublin's most cherished traditional pubs.

Highlights:
  • Explore the medieval ruins of Rock of Dunamase with panoramic countryside views
  • Visit Ireland's highest waterfall at Powerscourt Estate
  • Discover the ancient monastic city of Glendalough
  • Experience authentic Irish music at The Cobblestone and Darkey Kelly's
