Daniels Family Niagara Falls Vacation

July 13-15, 2025 | Custom Travel Proposal

Trip Overview

Experience the best of Niagara Falls with this 3-day family vacation featuring falls experience tours, scenic drives, and exploration of surrounding areas. Your package includes flights from Pensacola to Buffalo via Atlanta, accommodations at the Sheraton Fallsview Hotel, and an Alamo SUV rental to give you the freedom to explore at your own pace.

Highlights:

  • Experience the magnificent Niagara Falls from both Canadian and US perspectives
  • Enjoy a comprehensive guided tour including Skylon Tower, Journey Behind the Falls, and Hornblower Cruise
  • Witness the spectacular illumination and fireworks displays
  • Explore the charming heritage district of Niagara-on-the-Lake
  • Take a scenic drive with stops at iconic landmarks along the Niagara River
