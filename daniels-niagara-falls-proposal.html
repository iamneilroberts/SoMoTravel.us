Dining Tips & Recommendations

  • Reservations: Make dinner reservations early, especially for window/view seating. Call directly rather than using third-party apps for special requests.
  • Early Dining Specials: Most high-end restaurants offer early dining specials (5-6:30pm) with significant discounts on the same quality meals.
  • Family-Friendly: All recommended restaurants welcome families with teens. Mention when booking that you'll have a 17-year-old dining with you.
  • Menu Flexibility: Most restaurants can modify dishes for preferences and offer half portions for lighter appetites.
  • Canadian Specialties: Try local Niagara wines - many restaurants feature excellent selections at reasonable prices.
  • Portions: Canadian restaurant portions tend to be generous. Consider sharing appetizers family-style.
  • Valet Parking: Available at Skylon Tower for convenience.
  • Timing: For the best experience at view restaurants, time your reservation around sunset or for the evening illumination.
  • Border Crossing Note: The original Anchor Bar is on the US side in Buffalo - perfect for your arrival lunch!
