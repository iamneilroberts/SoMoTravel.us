Secret Garden at Chester Beatty Library

Escape the Dublin crowds at this hidden rooftop garden with stunning views of Dublin Castle. The library itself houses an incredible collection of ancient manuscripts and is completely free to visit.

FREE ATTRACTION HIDDEN SPOT
Dun Laoghaire Coastal Walk & James Joyce Tower

Take advantage of your rental car for a beautiful coastal drive to Dun Laoghaire, just 30 minutes from Dublin. Walk the pier for spectacular views of Dublin Bay and visit the James Joyce Tower in nearby Sandycove (featured in Ulysses).

SCENIC DRIVE LOCAL FAVORITE
Hidden London: Postman's Park

Near St. Paul's Cathedral lies this serene little park with a unique memorial - the Watts Memorial to Heroic Self-Sacrifice, commemorating ordinary people who died saving others. A moving and quiet spot to reflect away from London's crowds.

FREE ATTRACTION HISTORICAL
Borough Market Food Experience

London's oldest food market is perfect for lunch during your London stay. Skip expensive restaurants and create your own gourmet meal from the market's incredible vendors. Don't miss the free food samples throughout!

LOCAL FOOD BUDGET-FRIENDLY
