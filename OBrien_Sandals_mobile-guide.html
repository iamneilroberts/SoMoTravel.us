Dining Recommendations

Exchange Privileges

Guests have access to dine at all 27 restaurants across the three Sandals Resorts in St. Lucia. Complimentary shuttles run every 2 hours between resorts.

Resort Dining Highlights
Gordon's on the Pier
Over-water fine dining • Seafood & Steak • Reservations Required

An exclusive dining experience on a pier over the water, specializing in seafood and steak. Make reservations early, especially for sunset dining. Perfect for a romantic dinner.

Bombay
Indian Cuisine • Dinner Only

Highly-rated Indian restaurant offering spicy and flavorful dishes. Popular menu items include Lamb Chop starter and Chicken Dhaba Curry.

Bayside Restaurant
International Cuisine • Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner

Serves buffet-style meals throughout the day and transforms into a French Brasserie in the evening. Great for breakfast with views of the main pool.

The Jerk Shack
Caribbean • Lunch

A casual favorite for lunch serving authentic Caribbean jerk chicken. A perfect stop after a morning of beach activities.

Soy Sushi Bar
Japanese • Dinner Only

Traditional sushi restaurant offering fresh sushi options for dinner in an intimate setting.

Kimonos
Japanese Teppanyaki • Dinner Only

Japanese Teppanyaki-style restaurant where chefs cook your meal right in front of you, providing both entertainment and delicious food.

Olde London Pub
English Pub • Lunch & Dinner • Open Late

Located just off the lobby, a great spot for late-night drinks and classic English pub fare. One of the few dining venues open past 10 PM.
