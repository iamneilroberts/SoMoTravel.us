Local Transportation Guide

London Transportation

  • Oyster Card: Recommended for public transport (£5 deposit, top up as needed)
  • London Underground (Tube): Primary mode of city transportation
  • Daily cap: £8.10 for zones 1-2
  • Buses accept Oyster Card and contactless payments
  • Download apps: Citymapper or TfL Go for real-time transit info

Paris Transportation

  • Paris Visite Travel Pass: Recommended for tourists
  • Options: 1-5 day passes covering metro, bus, RER
  • Price range: €14.90 - €65.80 depending on duration and zones
  • Metro and RER train network extensive and efficient
  • Download app: RATP for route planning

Vienna Transportation

  • Vienna City Card: Includes public transport and discounts
  • 24-hour pass: €17.10 (includes airport transfer)
  • Comprehensive network: U-Bahn (subway), trams, and buses
  • Most attractions accessible by public transport
  • Download app: WienMobil for real-time schedules

Rome Transportation

  • Roma Pass: Public transport and museum access
  • 48-hour pass: €32.00 (includes 2 museum entries)
  • Metro limited, buses and trams more extensive
  • Walking often fastest in historic center
  • Beware of pickpockets on public transport
  • Download app: Moovit for route planning

Important Notes

  • UK ETA required for London (£10 pp, apply 3+ days before)
  • EU ETIAS required (€7 pp, apply 30+ days before)
  • All restaurant reservations must be made in advance
  • Local taxes additional at hotels
  • Tours include skip-the-line access
