  • Recommended return: 7:50 PM (arrives 9:32 PM)

Suggested London Itinerary

Total time in London: ~10 hours (10:00 AM - 7:30 PM)

  • 10:30 AM - 12:00 PM: Westminster Abbey
    Gothic architecture, Poets' Corner
    2-minute walk from Westminster tube station
  • 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM: Thames riverside walk
    Parliament photo opportunities
    Big Ben, London Eye views
  • 1:00 PM - 3:00 PM: Tower of London
    Crown Jewels, medieval palace
    20-minute tube journey from Westminster
  • 3:30 PM - 5:00 PM: British Museum
    Rosetta Stone, Egyptian mummies
    15-minute tube journey from Tower Hill
  • 5:15 PM - 6:45 PM: St Paul's Cathedral
    Dome climb for sunset views
    15-minute walk from British Museum
  • 6:45 PM - 7:30 PM: Evening South Bank walk
    Riverside views, dinner options
    Short walk across Millennium Bridge from St Paul's

Travel Tips:

  • Purchase train tickets in advance for best prices
  • Consider getting a one-day London Travelcard with your train ticket
  • Book Westminster Abbey and Tower of London tickets online to avoid queues
  • British Museum is free entry, no booking required
  • Last entry to St Paul's dome is typically 4:15 PM
