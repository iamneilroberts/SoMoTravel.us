Print & Tracking Notes

  • QR code encodes: https://somo.voygent.ai/?src=print-ad
  • Tracking: The ?src=print-ad parameter is captured by the landing page and stored in KV at _somo/sources/print-ad. Daily visit counts at _somo/visits/{date}.
  • Per-newspaper tracking: Use different src values per placement:
    • ?src=print-lagniappe — Lagniappe
    • ?src=print-courier — Mobile Press-Register
    • ?src=print-fairhope — Fairhope Courier
    Generate a unique QR for each with: qrencode -o qr-{name}.png -s 10 -l H "https://somo.voygent.ai/?src=print-{name}"
  • Size: 2" wide x 4" tall. Ads shown here at 150 DPI (300x600px). For print, export at 300 DPI.
  • QR minimum: QR code shown at ~0.6" — minimum scannable at 300 DPI newspaper resolution.
  • Colors: Pure black (#111) on white — safe for B&W newspaper print. Accent color (#1a5f7a) will render as dark gray in B&W.
  • Font: Helvetica/Arial — universally available, highly legible in newspaper print.
