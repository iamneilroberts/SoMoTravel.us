McLeod Heritage Journey: Highlands, Skye & Scenic Rail

7 Days • Inverness Home Base • No Driving Required

This adventure blends comfort and heritage: you’ll be based in Inverness, with small‑group day tours and train journeys unlocking Skye, Loch Ness, and the Highlands. It feels like a luxury holiday, but without large tour buses or complicated logistics. Clan connections are woven in, with visits to Dunvegan Castle and Culloden Battlefield.

For 2 Travelers

Total Package: $4,530 (about $2,265 per person)

What’s Included

  • Flights Gulfport → Inverness (with connections)
  • 5 nights at Kingsmills Hotel (Deluxe room, breakfast included)
  • Skye 2‑day / 1‑night small‑group tour (Portree overnight)
  • Loch Ness full‑day small‑group tour (with cruise & Urquhart Castle)
  • Train Day: choose from gentle heritage steam to adventurous scenic lines

Day‑by‑Day Itinerary

Day 1: Depart Gulfport around noon (overnight flight)

Day 2: Arrive Inverness ~9am, settle in. Explore Inverness Museum, Cathedral, Ness Islands. Optional: Culloden Battlefield Visitor Centre.

Day 3: Skye Tour Day 1 — travel via Eilean Donan Castle, arrive Portree. Overnight in Portree.

Day 4: Skye Tour Day 2 — explore Fairy Pools, Quiraing, Dunvegan Castle. Return to Inverness in the evening.

Day 5: Loch Ness full‑day tour — cruise Loch Ness, visit Urquhart Castle, enjoy Highland villages.

Day 6: Train Day — choose your adventure:
• Gentle: Strathspey Steam Railway with Boat of Garten stop
• Moderate: Highland Main Line to Pitlochry (Victorian town, distillery)
• Adventurous: Kyle Line to Kyle of Lochalsh (dramatic lochs & Skye views)
• Epic: Far North Line to Wick/Thurso (remote northern Highlands)

Day 7: Fly home from Inverness

Pricing reflects flights + hotel package and listed tours only. Meals, admissions, and personal expenses are not included. Itinerary order may adjust with weather and seasonal opening times (e.g., Dunvegan Castle is open spring–autumn).
