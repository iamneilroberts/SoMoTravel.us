OVERVIEW

Embark on an unforgettable journey through the heart of Ireland, where ancient traditions meet farm-fresh culinary experiences. This carefully crafted 10-day adventure combines Ireland's scenic beauty, rich heritage, and musical soul with an emphasis on authentic farm-to-table dining experiences.

You'll stay in handpicked accommodations that showcase Ireland's agricultural heritage and culinary prowess, from the world-renowned Ballymaloe House to intimate organic farm stays. Each evening brings opportunities to immerse yourself in traditional Irish music sessions at authentic local pubs – venues where locals gather to share songs passed down through generations.

This unique itinerary takes you beyond the typical tourist path, offering exclusive experiences like meeting local farmers, participating in farm tours, and enjoying meals prepared with ingredients harvested just steps from your table. Along the way, you'll discover breathtaking landscapes, historic castles, and peaceful waterways – all while connecting with the land and people who make Ireland truly special.

DETAILED ITINERARY

DAY 1-2: DUBLIN AREA | The Merry Ploughboy

Arrive in Dublin and begin your Irish adventure with traditional music and historic sites including Rock of Dunamase and Powerscourt Waterfall. Your first accommodation features its own traditional music venue.

DAY 3-6: CORK REGION | Ballymaloe House

Travel south to the culinary heart of Ireland. Your four-night stay at the world-renowned Ballymaloe House puts you at the center of Ireland's farm-to-table movement. Explore Cork city, the scenic Dingle and Beara Peninsulas, and enjoy nightly music sessions.

DAY 7-10: WESTMEATH | Rathcam Organic Farm

Journey to central Ireland's hidden gem region. Stay at Rathcam Organic Farm near Mullingar, birthplace of Irish traditional music organizations. Explore historic Athlone, tour the organic farm, and experience local music sessions. Your journey concludes with a visit to Clonmacnoise before departing from Dublin.

Ballymaloe House | Cork (4 nights)

€195/night - World-renowned country house hotel and restaurant on a 100-acre organic farm. The Allen family pioneered farm-to-table cuisine in Ireland. Elegant country rooms surrounded by gardens that supply the famous restaurant.

€115/night - Authentic 19th-century farmhouse on a certified organic farm run by a mother-daughter team. Located near Mullingar with easy access to traditional music venues. Experience true farm life with home-cooked meals from farm-fresh ingredients.
