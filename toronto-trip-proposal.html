What Makes This Trip Special

  • Urban Nature Escapes: Hike 25+ km of trails within Toronto - from lakefront bluffs to forest valleys
  • Live Music Heritage: Experience venues that launched Blue Rodeo, Barenaked Ladies, and countless others
  • Hidden Outdoor Gems: Scarborough Bluffs, Don Valley trails, Toronto Islands, Rouge National Urban Park
  • Antique Paradise: Hunt for treasures in Toronto's best vintage districts
  • Perfect Balance: Mix of outdoor adventures, cultural exploration, and urban discoveries
  • Off-the-Beaten-Path: Skip tourist areas for authentic neighborhood experiences and hidden trails

Outdoor Adventure Highlights

  • Scarborough Bluffs Trail: 5-6km lakefront hike with 90m high dramatic cliffs
  • Toronto Islands: 5km island loop trail with beaches and skyline views
  • Don Valley System: Network of trails through urban forest with wildlife sightings
  • Crothers Woods: 6.6km loop with mountain biking options and city vistas
  • Rouge National Urban Park: Canada's first urban national park with diverse ecosystems
  • High Park: 400 acres with trails through rare oak savannah habitat
  • Elora Gorge: Limestone canyon with riverside trails and historic village

All trails are moderate difficulty and suitable for active adults. Total hiking distance: approximately 30km over 5 days.
