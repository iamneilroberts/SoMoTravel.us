Detailed Itinerary

Sunday, Jun 13 Day 1 - Arrival & Toronto Introduction
Morning

Depart Mobile (American via Charlotte or Delta via Atlanta)

Arrive Buffalo Airport at approximately 12:30 PM

Afternoon

Pick up rental car and drive to Toronto via Lewiston

Stop for lunch at The Silo Restaurant (waterfront dining)

Cross border into Canada (90-minute drive to Toronto)

Evening

Check-in at The Gladstone House (West Queen West arts district)

Dinner at The Wren gastropub in the Danforth neighborhood

Live music at the legendary Cameron House Hidden Gem

Monday, Jun 14 Day 2 - Hamilton Waterfalls Adventure
Morning

Breakfast at The Lakeview Restaurant (classic Toronto diner since 1932)

Drive to Spencer Gorge Conservation Area

Hike to Dundas Peak and Tew Falls (41m tallest waterfall) Photo Op

Afternoon

Lunch at The Burnt Tongue in Dundas

Visit Sherman Falls and Canterbury Falls (easy access waterfalls)

Craft beer tasting at Merit Brewing Company

Evening

Early dinner at The Ship gastropub with live music

Return to Toronto

Nightcap at The Dakota Tavern (roots & bluegrass music)

Tuesday, Jun 15 Day 3 - Elora Gorge & Neighborhoods
Morning

Coffee at Saving Gigi (Parkdale local roastery)

Drive to Elora (90 minutes through Ontario countryside)

Explore Victoria Park and Elora Gorge viewpoints Photo Op

Afternoon

Hike Lover's Leap trail along limestone cliffs

Lunch at The Friendly Society (British gastropub)

Quick tasting at Elora Brewing Company

Evening

Return to Toronto, explore Little Portugal neighborhood

Cocktails at Bar Poet

Dinner at The Federal gastropub with live music

Late show at The Garrison indie music venue

Wednesday, Jun 16 Day 4 - Islands & Departure
Morning

Check out and breakfast at The Stockyards

Ferry to Ward's Island

Walk quiet island streets with skyline views Hidden Gem

Light lunch at The Rectory Café

Afternoon

Return ferry to mainland

Drive to Buffalo with quick stop at Niagara Falls (US side)

Return rental car at Buffalo Airport

Evening

6:30 PM departure back to Mobile
