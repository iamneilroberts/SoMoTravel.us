Must-Do Adventures

1. Yellowstone Snowmobile Tour

  • Price: $325/person
  • Duration: Full day (11am-5pm)
  • Experience the winter wonderland of Yellowstone on a guided snowmobile adventure
  • Visit Old Faithful, Madison River Valley, and spot winter wildlife
  • Book 2+ weeks in advance - sells out quickly!

2. Dog Sledding Experience

  • Price: $85/person (group rate for 4+: $75/person)
  • Duration: 1-1.5 hours
  • Learn about mushing and meet the sled dogs
  • Add-on mushing lesson available: $25/person

3. Skiing/Snowboarding

  • Snow King Mountain (in town):
    • Lift ticket: $89/day | Rentals: $65/day
    • Perfect for mixed skill levels
    • Night skiing available
  • Jackson Hole Mountain Resort:
    • Lift ticket: $165/day | Rentals: $85/day
    • World-class terrain
