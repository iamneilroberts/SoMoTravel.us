Altitude Tips

High Altitude Precautions
  • Breckenridge sits at 9,600 feet elevation
  • Stay hydrated! Drink more water than you think you need
  • Limit alcohol consumption, especially on your first day
  • Consider starting with half-day activities to acclimate
  • Symptoms of altitude sickness include headache, nausea, and fatigue
  • If symptoms persist, seek medical attention
Kim's Local Tips

Morning Ski Strategy: Start early! The first hour after lifts open has the best snow conditions and shortest lines.

Non-Ski Day Option: If Jennifer wants to join for part of a ski day, the gondola is scenic ride-friendly for non-skiers.

Hidden Local Secret: The oxygen bar on Main Street can be a lifesaver if altitude symptoms hit!
