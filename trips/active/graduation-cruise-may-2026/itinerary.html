‚ö†Ô∏è Important Notes for Progreso

Chichen Itza is ~2 hours each way. It's a long day but WORTH IT for this once-in-a-lifetime experience!

$5 USD fee for video equipment inside ruins. Bring extra for souvenirs and tips.

Lots of walking on uneven terrain. Skip the sandals for this one!

Book Ship or Guaranteed

This is one port where booking a guaranteed-return excursion is CRITICAL due to the long drive.
