UK ETA Application Guide

MANDATORY: UK Electronic Travel Authorization Required
Apply by April 15, 2026 - ¬£10 per person

What is the UK ETA?

The UK Electronic Travel Authorization (ETA) is mandatory for US citizens visiting the UK, including Scotland. This became required on January 8, 2025.

Application Details

When to Apply:

By April 15, 2026 - Two weeks before travel

Cost:

  • ¬£10 per person (approximately $13)
  • Total for your group: ¬£30 ($39)
  • Must apply separately for each traveler

What You Need:

  1. Valid US passport for each traveler
  2. Email address
  3. Payment card (credit/debit)
  4. Travel dates

How to Apply

  1. Visit: https://www.gov.uk/guidance/apply-for-an-electronic-travel-authorisation-eta
  2. Click "Apply for an ETA"
  3. Complete application for EACH traveler
  4. Pay ¬£10 per application
  5. Receive approval (usually within minutes)
  6. Save and print confirmations

Your ETA Covers:

  • ‚úÖ Edinburgh arrival (April 30)
  • ‚úÖ Your entire Scotland stay
  • ‚úÖ London Heathrow transit (May 5)
  • Valid for 2 years for multiple trips

Ireland - No ETIAS Required

  • Ireland is NOT in the Schengen Area
  • Ireland does NOT require ETIAS
  • US citizens enter visa-free
  • Just need valid passport

Brussels & Paris Transit

Your flights transit through Brussels and Paris airports. These are just airport transits - no immigration, so no ETIAS needed.

Remember: No UK ETA = No entry to Scotland!
