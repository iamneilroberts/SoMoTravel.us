Trip Description

A customized journey through Ireland and England with special focus on historical sites, castles, and equestrian experiences. This trip combines leisure travel with a work-related component, allowing you to experience the rich cultural heritage of Ireland, a taste of London, and professional engagement in Stoneleigh.

Trip Highlights

  • Explore historic Dublin with its castles and vibrant culture
  • Visit the Irish National Stud & Gardens – Ireland’s premier horse breeding facility
  • Discover ancient sites including the Hill of Tara and Glendalough Monastic Site
  • See London’s iconic landmarks including the Tower of London
  • Mix your farrier work in Stoneleigh with adventures in the beautiful Warwickshire countryside
  • Self-drive exploration allowing flexibility and authentic experiences
