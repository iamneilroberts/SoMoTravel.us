üìã First-Timer Tips

For Paris:

  • Learn basic French phrases (locals appreciate effort)
  • Dinner starts late (8-9 PM)
  • Keep hotel business card for taxi returns
  • Museum Pass worth it for 3+ museums
  • Validate metro tickets before boarding

For London:

  • Oyster Card or contactless for all transport
  • Stand right on escalators
  • Pubs close early (11 PM)
  • Many museums free (donations welcomed)
  • Weather changeable - bring layers

Money Matters:

  • Notify banks of travel
  • Both cities prefer cards over cash
  • Tipping: 10-15% restaurants, round up for taxis
  • ATMs widely available

Documents Needed:

  • Passports valid 6+ months
  • No visa for US citizens (under 90 days)
  • Consider Global Entry for faster returns
  • Travel insurance documentation
  • Hotel confirmations printed/downloaded
