Afternoon — Hidden Gem
✨ El Call: Barcelona's Ancient Jewish Quarter Optional
Available if you have free time
Tucked inside the Gothic Quarter, El Call was one of the most important Jewish communities in medieval Europe before the expulsions of 1391 and 1492. The Sinagoga Major de Barcelona — one of the oldest surviving synagogues in Europe, dating to Roman times — is hidden down a narrow alley on Carrer de Marlet. Easy to miss, extraordinary to find. Small, intimate, and deeply moving. The MUHBA El Call museum next door tells the full story of Jewish life in medieval Barcelona. Perfect for Jonathan and father-in-law. Melinda will love the mystical, hidden-alley energy of the quarter.
