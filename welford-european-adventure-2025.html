Pre-Trip Checklist

Required Before Departure:

  • Apply for UK ETA ($10 per person) at least 30 days before travel
  • Apply for EU ETIAS ($7 per person) at least 30 days before travel
  • Check passport validity (must be valid for at least 6 months beyond return date)
  • Make restaurant reservations in advance (particularly for Paris)
  • Notify credit card companies of travel plans
  • Arrange for international data plan or purchase local SIM cards
  • Download offline maps for London, Paris, and Rome
  • Exchange some currency before departure (£, €)

Night-Before Checklist:

  • Check flight status
  • Complete online check-in for American Airlines (if available)
  • Pack medications in carry-on luggage
  • Ensure all electronics are charged
  • Set alarm for departure day (leave for airport 3 hours before flight)

Local Customs & Etiquette

Tipping:

  • UK: 10-15% in restaurants (check if service charge is already included), £1-2 for taxis
  • France: Service charge is included by law. Leave small change for good service.
  • Italy: Service charge ("coperto") often included. Additional 5-10% for exceptional service.

Transportation:

  • London: Oyster card or contactless payment for all public transit. Stand on right on escalators.
  • Paris: Metro tickets can be purchased in books of 10 ("carnet") for savings.
  • Rome: Validate bus/tram tickets before boarding. Taxis should be taken from official stands.
