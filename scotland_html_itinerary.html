Scotland Heritage Journey: Edinburgh, Highlands & Skye

10 Days • Edinburgh, Glasgow, Inverness & Isle of Skye • No Rental Car Required
This comprehensive adventure showcases Scotland's most iconic destinations using excellent public transportation and a private driver on Skye. You'll experience ancient castles, Highland scenery, whisky heritage, and dramatic island landscapes. Perfect for active 60+ travelers seeking comfort, culture, and scenic beauty without driving stress.
$3,700-5,200 for 2 Travelers (about $1,850-2,600 per person)
Day 1: Travel Day
Mobile, Alabama → Edinburgh, Scotland
  • Morning: Depart Mobile (via Atlanta or Charlotte)
  • Route Options: Delta/Virgin or American/British Airways via London
  • Evening: Arrive Edinburgh, check into Royal Mile area hotel
Days 2-4: Edinburgh Discovery
3 nights in Scotland's historic capital
  • Day 2: Edinburgh Castle (timed entry), Royal Mile walk, St. Giles' Cathedral, traditional Scottish dinner
  • Day 3: Princes Street shopping, National Gallery, Calton Hill panoramic views, ghost walking tour
  • Day 4: Day trip choice - Stirling Castle & Wallace Monument OR historic St. Andrews (both accessible by train)
Days 5-6: Glasgow Culture
2 nights in Scotland's vibrant cultural hub
  • Day 5: 1-hour train from Edinburgh, Glasgow Cathedral, Necropolis Hill, West End dining
  • Day 6: Kelvingrove Art Gallery, University of Glasgow, Ashton Lane, traditional pub experience
Days 7-8: Highland Gateway
2 nights in Inverness
  • Day 7: Scenic 3.5-hour Highland train to Inverness, Inverness Castle, River Ness walk
  • Day 8: Full-day Loch Ness tour including Urquhart Castle ruins, loch cruise, Highland villages
Day 9: Isle of Skye with Private Driver
Inverness → Skye → Inverness (Private driver on island)
  • Early Morning: 3-hour bus journey to Portree via scenic Highland roads
  • 10:30 AM: Meet private driver for comprehensive island tour
  • Tour Highlights: Portree harbor, Old Man of Storr dramatic rock formations, Kilt Rock coastal cliffs, Dunvegan Castle (MacLeod clan seat), optional Talisker Distillery
  • 6:00 PM: Return to Portree for evening bus back to Inverness
Day 10: Return Journey
Inverness → Edinburgh → Mobile
  • Morning: Final Highland train journey to Edinburgh (3.5 hours)
  • Afternoon: International flight connections home
  • Evening: Arrive Mobile with unforgettable memories
