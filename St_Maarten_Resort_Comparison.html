üí° Our Recommendations

For a modern boutique experience with stunning pool: The Morgan Resort & Spa offers a brand-new property (2021), exceptional service, and one of the island's best infinity pools. Walking distance to Maho Beach and ~5 min drive to Mullet Bay.
For all-inclusive family value near Mullet Bay: The Sonesta Maho Beach Resort offers the best balance of location (5-minute drive), all-inclusive convenience, and family-friendly amenities.
For a luxury romantic getaway (adults): The Sonesta Ocean Point delivers an exceptional adults-only experience with gourmet dining and premium service, also just 5 minutes from Mullet Bay.
For a beautiful private beach experience: The Divi Little Bay offers a stunning peninsula setting with calm waters ideal for swimming and snorkeling. Note: 20-25 minutes from Mullet Bay.
For budget-conscious travelers: The Sapphire Beach Club provides excellent value with kitchen facilities, though guests should be prepared for a dated property and short drives to beaches.
