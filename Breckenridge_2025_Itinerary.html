üåü BONUS SUGGESTIONS - FREE ACTIVITIES

1. Sapphire Point Overlook

Perfect for: Morning or sunset visits, easy family walk

  • Breathtaking views of Lake Dillon and surrounding mountains
  • Flat, easy loop trail with paved and gravel walkways - accessible for all ages
  • Wildlife sightings including friendly chipmunks (bring sunflower seeds!)
  • Benches and picnic tables for relaxing
  • Incredible photo opportunities, especially at sunrise/sunset
  • Location: 15 minutes from Breckenridge via Swan Mountain Road
  • Cost: FREE - no parking fees
2. Carter Park Sledding Hill

Perfect for: Afternoon fun when taking a ski break

  • Free sledding hill with beautiful mountain views
  • Great for families, friends, and dogs
  • Sleds available on-site (first-come, first-served honor system)
  • Bring your own sled if you prefer
  • Relaxing atmosphere for all ages
  • Location: High Street & Wellington Road, Breckenridge
  • Cost: FREE - no equipment rental required
3. Blue Lakes Trailhead - Mountain Goat Viewing

Perfect for: Wildlife enthusiasts and nature photographers

  • 20-minute scenic drive from Breckenridge
  • Excellent opportunity to see mountain goats in their natural habitat
  • Stunning alpine lake views
  • Easy viewing from parking area or short hike options
  • Best viewing: Early morning or late afternoon
  • Location: Blue Lakes Trailhead (via Highway 9 South)
  • Cost: FREE
  • Tip: Bring binoculars and camera with zoom lens
