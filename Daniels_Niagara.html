  • Historic brewery housed in a converted church with unique atmosphere
  • Sample craft beers and enjoy live music (if available)
  • Tour the brewing facilities and learn about the brewing process
  • Cost: CAD $15-25 per person for tastings

OPTION 2: Niagara College Teaching Winery & Brewery

  • Educational experience at Canada's first teaching winery
  • Tour the vineyards and brewing facilities
  • Tastings of student-produced wines and craft beers
  • Beautiful campus grounds with panoramic views
  • Cost: CAD $15-20 per person for tastings

Visit Website

OPTION 3: Water Sports at Port Dalhousie

  • After visiting the beach area in the afternoon, extend your time there
  • Rent paddle boards or kayaks to explore the harbor
  • Great way to get active and enjoy the scenery from the water
  • Cost: CAD $25-40 per hour for rentals

Visit Paddle Niagara

OPTION 4: Hiking at Short Hills Provincial Park

  • Scenic hiking trails through Niagara's most rugged terrain
  • Beautiful waterfalls and diverse wildlife
  • Moderate to challenging trails for more active young adults
  • Cost: Free (parking fee may apply)

Visit Ontario Parks Website
