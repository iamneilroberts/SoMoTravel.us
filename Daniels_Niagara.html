The Distillery District (Toronto)

One of Toronto's most vibrant areas, the Distillery District is a pedestrian-only village of brick-lined streets and beautifully restored Victorian industrial buildings. This National Historic Site features distinctive art galleries, boutiques, and some of Toronto's best restaurants and cafes in a uniquely preserved setting.
