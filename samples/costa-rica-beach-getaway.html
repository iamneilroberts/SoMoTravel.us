Hidden Gems

Tarcoles Bridge Crocodiles

Free roadside attraction on the way from the airport - dozens of massive American crocodiles basking below

Pro Tip: Pull off on the Jaco side, not the San José side, for safer parking

Playa Hermosa

Just 10 minutes south of Jaco - less crowded, better surf, more local vibe

Pro Tip: Great for watching pro surfers, not ideal for swimming (strong currents)

Pura Vida Waterfall Gardens

Private waterfall just outside Jaco - swim in a jungle pool without the tourist crowds

Pro Tip: Ask locals for directions, it's not well-marked
